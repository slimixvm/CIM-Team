What is your job?

As a CIM representative, you are responsible for various tasks such as resolving Data Quality Problems (DQP), Searching and Approving, Company creation, and providing ticket support. See the guide pages for more information.

Every month, we are given a set of work tasks that we need to complete at 100% in order to be eligible for a bonus in our salary. If we exceed expectations and complete the tasks at 125%, we have the opportunity to earn even more money based on our rank.The performance metric that we are discussing is commonly referred to as a KPI, or Key Performance Indicator.A Key Performance Indicator (KPI) is a measurable value that helps an individual, team, or organization track progress towards specific goals or objectives. KPIs are used to evaluate performance and determine whether the desired outcomes are being achieved. By setting and monitoring KPIs, organizations can make data-driven decisions to improve their operations and achieve their targets.
