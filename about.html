Who are we?

The CIM Team currently operates as a subdivision of the PIM department, which is in charge of managing product and company databases. This team provides informational support to clients and MTEAM departments. At present, the creation, search, and management of companies are all tasks handled by the CIM department. In the near future, the CIM department is anticipated to .(details coming up soon).

Our scope of work extends beyond company management; we also concentrate on automating processes within our own department and helping colleagues from other departments complete tasks more efficiently. We are always eager to embrace new ideas that can enhance the system and optimize the overall work process, making it quicker and more intelligent.

Additionally, we wholeheartedly welcome individuals who are passionate about programming and can help improve our daily work through scripts and innovative solutions that streamline the workflow.

Our primary mission at this time is to demonstrate to the board of directors that we are well-prepared to shoulder more responsibilities. We possess the knowledge, drive, and determination to consistently deliver outstanding results. Our team has already accomplished a great deal to earn this recognition, such as creating comprehensive guides for the entire Grafit Holding, developing scripts that streamline work across multiple departments, and building this website entirely in-house without assistance from other teams. We are confident that our achievements thus far clearly indicate that we deserve the opportunity to play a more significant role at Grafit Holding.
